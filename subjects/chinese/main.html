静夜思

李白

床前明月光，

疑是地上霜。

举头望明月，

低头思故乡。

Analysis

This famous poem by Li Bai expresses the poet's homesickness under the moonlight. The moonlight that shines on his bed reminds him of frost, and as he looks up at the moon, he thinks of his homeland.
