Physics Formula Reference

Quick access to essential physics formulas

Mechanics Formulas

Newton's Second Law

$$\vec{F} = m\vec{a}$$

The force acting on an object is equal to the mass of the object multiplied by its acceleration.

Kinetic Energy

$$E_k = \frac{1}{2}mv^2$$

The energy an object possesses due to its motion.

Electromagnetic Formulas

Coulomb's Law

$$F = k_e \frac{q_1 q_2}{r^2}$$

The electrostatic force between two charged particles.

Ohm's Law

$$V = IR$$

The relationship between voltage, current, and resistance in an electrical circuit.
