Formula Reference

Quick access to common mathematical formulas

Algebra Formulas

Quadratic Formula

$$x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$$

For a quadratic equation \(ax^2 + bx + c = 0\), this formula gives the solutions.

Binomial Theorem

$$(a + b)^n = \sum_{k=0}^{n} {n \choose k} a^{n-k} b^k$$

Expands the power of a binomial expression.

Calculus Formulas

Power Rule (Derivatives)

$$\frac{d}{dx}[x^n] = nx^{n-1}$$

The derivative of a variable raised to a power.

Fundamental Theorem of Calculus

$$\int_a^b f(x) \, dx = F(b) - F(a)$$

Where F(x) is an antiderivative of f(x).
